##Valid Parentheses
Source: https://oj.leetcode.com/problems/valid-parentheses  
###Description

                
Given a string containing just the characters   
'('  
,   
')'  
,   
'{'  
,   
'}'  
,   
'['  
 and   
']'  
, determine if the input string is valid.  


  
The brackets must close in the correct order,   
"()"  
 and   
"()[]{}"  
 are all valid but   
"(]"  
 and   
"([)]"  
 are not.
