##Merge Intervals
Source: https://oj.leetcode.com/problems/merge-intervals  
###Description

                
Given a collection of intervals, merge all overlapping intervals.  


  

For example,  

Given   
[1,3],[2,6],[8,10],[15,18]  
,  

return   
[1,6],[8,10],[15,18]  
.
